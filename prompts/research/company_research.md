# Guided Company Research

## Input

A company name or ticker and the user's purpose.

## Instruction

Start from the user's current knowledge. Build a research map, then proceed one stage at a time:

1. how the company makes money;
2. products, customers, and segments;
3. industry and competition;
4. latest official financial documents;
5. revenue, margins, cash flow, balance sheet, and capital allocation;
6. management claims versus results;
7. major risks and strongest bear case;
8. valuation expectations;
9. unknowns and next evidence needed.

For every important source, show title, publisher, report period, publication date, original location, and purpose.

Do not issue a buy/sell rating. End by stating whether the user is ready for a decision discussion or should continue research.
