# Decision Review

Use the original decision record and only information available at that time to evaluate process quality.

Then separately introduce later facts.

Review:

- problem definition;
- evidence quality;
- missing information;
- opposing evidence;
- position sizing and alternatives;
- emotional or cognitive bias;
- whether rules were followed;
- which outcomes came from judgment versus luck;
- one or two reusable lessons.

Do not rewrite the original record. Produce rule changes only as user-confirmed candidates.
