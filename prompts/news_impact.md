# News Impact Review

Given a news item and, when relevant, the user's confirmed position or thesis:

1. verify the original event and source;
2. distinguish event date from publication date;
3. remove duplicate reporting and market commentary;
4. identify what is genuinely new;
5. explain which existing assumption it supports, weakens, contradicts, or does not affect;
6. identify alternative explanations;
7. state whether the news changes fundamentals, risk, valuation expectations, or only short-term sentiment;
8. recommend research next steps, not automatic trading action.

If no material thesis change exists, say so clearly and keep the output brief.
