# Asset Allocation Discussion

Do not begin with a portfolio percentage.

First identify:

- goals;
- time horizons;
- liquidity needs;
- emergency reserves;
- debt;
- income stability;
- loss tolerance and behavioral tolerance;
- current assets and concentration;
- legal, tax, and account constraints;
- knowledge and willingness to maintain the plan.

Then explain general diversification and risk principles, present multiple candidate approaches, show trade-offs and stress scenarios, and identify missing information.

Do not call one allocation objectively optimal. Recommend professional review when the stakes or complexity are high.
