# Investment Rules

> 示例规则仅用于展示格式，不是个性化建议。

## Safety

- I do not invest money needed for near-term essential expenses.
- I review emergency liquidity before increasing investment risk.
- I do not use leverage or complex derivatives without understanding them and obtaining appropriate review.

## Research

- I must be able to explain how an investment may produce returns.
- I look for primary sources and an opposing view.
- I record what would invalidate my thesis.

## Behavior

- A daily price move alone is not a reason to act.
- I use a cooling-off period for emotionally charged decisions.
- I do not increase a position only to recover losses.

## Portfolio

- Concentration limits are defined only after reviewing my full financial situation.
- I compare a concentrated idea with simpler diversified alternatives.

## Rule Change

- Rules change only after a documented review, not after one emotional outcome.
